Educational Game

I.T Genius is a game similar to 4pics 1 word, whereas in 4pics 1 word you'll get to form a word the given scrambled letters, here you'll provide your own letters to form word. Each difficulty consists of 10 pictures. Each level can be played for multiple times or until the players perfected the scores.

1.

First, select a difficulty, either easy, medium or hard.

2.

The player's aim is to guess what word does the 4 images wish to convey.


3.

Remember that the answers from different levels is related to computers and programming.

4.

Each successive answer will increase the score by one point and each unsuccessful answer will not increase the score. The score will be seen from the upper left along with the total scores if the answer are perfect.


5.

You can repeat each level how many times you want or atleast until you have perfected it.


6.

Enjoy!
